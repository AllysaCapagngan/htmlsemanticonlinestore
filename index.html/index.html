About Us

In a strategic move to enhance its offerings, Fab Wear Apparel has secured a partnership with Shein, a leading brand in the Philippines. This collaboration brings a wealth of experience and fashion-forward products to the store, ensuring a diverse and stylish range of apparel for our discerning clientele.

Fab Wear Apparel is an innovative retail establishment specializing in unique and trendy clothing, curated for the dynamic tastes of the Gen Z demographic. Founded by Allysa's Team, recent graduates of Saint James High School with a dedicated focus on entrepreneurship, the business is the result of a comprehensive Business Plan developed during their academic pursuits.
